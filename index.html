ABOUT

Chainsaw Man follows the story of Denji, an impoverished young man who makes a contract that fuses his body with that of a dog-like devil named Pochita, granting him the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws. Denji eventually joins the Public Safety Devil Hunters, a government agency focused on fighting against devils whenever they become a threat to Japan. The second arc of the story focuses on Asa Mitaka, a high school student who enters into a contract with Yoru, the War Devil, who forces her to hunt down Chainsaw Man in order to reclaim the devils stolen from her.

CHARACTERS

Denji

Denji (デンジ?) is the titular protagonist of the Chainsaw Man manga and anime series. He is the main protagonist of the Public Safety Saga and the deuteragonist of the Academy Saga. As a young boy, he inherits his father's debts from the Yakuza. After meeting Pochita, he becomes a Devil Hunter for the Yakuza in an attempt to clear his debt. Once the Yakuza, under the control of a Devil, betrays him, he dies and Pochita becomes his heart. He is then revived and can become the Chainsaw Man (チェンソーマン Chensō Man?) by pulling a cord on his chest.

Aki Hayakawa

Aki Hayakawa (早はや川かわ アキ Hayakawa Aki?) was the deuteragonist of the Public Safety Saga. He was a Public Safety Devil Hunter working under Makima's special squad[1] who had contracts with the Curse Devil and Future Devil, and a former contract with the Fox Devil. During the Gun Devil arc, he was killed and possessed by 20% of the Gun Devil, thus becoming the host of the Gun Fiend

Power

Power (パワー Pawā?) was the tritagonist of the Public Safety Saga. She was the Blood Fiend (血ちの魔ま人じん Chi no Majin?) and a Public Safety Devil Hunter who was part of Makima's special squad.

Makima

Makima (マキマ?) was the main antagonist of the Public Safety Saga. She was a high-ranking Public Safety Devil Hunter who took Denji in as her human pet. She is later revealed to be the Control Devil (支し配はいの悪あく魔ま Shihai no Akuma?) who embodies the fear of control or conquest. Following her death, she is reincarnated as Nayuta..
